OBJECTIVES:
============================================================


We will need to complete the entire accounting structure in as much detail as possible on initial setup.

Use KNETOSCOPE INCORPORATED as a guide for the other companies - it is a legacy structure built over 20 years of business, and has just about everything I needed to run that company.

That being said, it is missing a few components, because it was a stand-alone company that is now being rolled into the RIGD_HOLDINGS  corporate structure, but will remain separate, with the intention of closing Knetoscope down by the end of the year - so it is not to me modified, only used as a guide for the setup of the other companies - especially GARCIA-DUNN STUDIO
